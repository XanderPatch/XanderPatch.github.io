Intro

Photo by Lili Popper on Unsplash

I embarked on my professional journey in the dynamic environment of Booktopia's Distribution Center in Sydney. As a DC Inventory Coordinator, I quickly distinguished myself by meticulously managing stock accuracy and implementing robust accounting processes. My acute analytical thinking and unwavering commitment to maintaining a safe and efficient work environment enabled me to reshape various inventory systems and streamline procedures. From managing bulk orders and investigating inventory discrepancies to handling special requests from customer service, I consistently demonstrated my ability to tackle complex challenges with precision, dedication, and innovative solutions.

In 2023, I transitioned into a critical role as the User Acceptance Test Coordinator for Project Next Gen at Booktopia. This project was a major initiative involving the transition to a new fulfilment centre and warehouse management system, and the integration of autonomous mobile robots. I developed comprehensive UAT plans and coordinated with diverse cross-functional teams and vendors to ensure seamless testing processes. My capacity to create detailed test cases covering all functional and non-functional aspects of the project was instrumental in guiding end-users through the testing phase. Leading defect triage sessions and collaborating with developers to resolve issues showcased my strong analytical skills and my talent for fostering effective teamwork. My efforts culminated in successful transitions to live operations, ensuring that end-users were well-prepared and supported.

Now, I excel as a QA Engineer at Booktopia, where I continually strive to elevate the quality of software releases. By crafting and executing meticulous test plans and strategies, I ensure that every software release meets the highest standards. My expertise in automation, utilizing tools like Selenium, has significantly increased testing efficiency and accuracy. In my role, I am dedicated to identifying and resolving software defects, conducting rigorous regression testing to maintain product stability. My ongoing commitment to excellence and continuous improvement has made me a valuable asset to Booktopia, driving innovation and efficiency in every project I undertake.
